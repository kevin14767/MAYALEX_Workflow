An English-Spanish-Ch’olti’ Word List

THe word list is the portion of the Philadelphia Manuscript that has received the greatest amount of scholarly attention. It contains a wealth of information about the Ch’olti’ language and the Ch’olti’ people. We have included English glosses to aid the interested reader with understanding the, at times, archaic Spanish, as well as the Ch’olti’. Original manuscript pages are indicated above the Spanish entry that begins that manuscript page, and all blank pages in the manuscript are noted.
